Welcome to The Little Saguaro Chamber of Commerce

We are here to hear, advocate, and nurish businesses within our community.

Here in The Little Saguaro Chamber of Commerce we primarly seek to inspire and bolster small businesses here in the town of Sahuarita. We provide events to hear out businesses and then plan for to best growth that these businesses can achieve which in turn helps our small community here in Southern Arizona to grow and flourish as the cacti of our town's namesake.

This is the logo for my site. I found the photo then edited text into it.

My Target Audiences

My primary audience of my Chamber of Commerce website will be for persons looking to learn more about the local Chamber of Commerce. They can learn about its history and location. Then it will have main members that can be contacted and reached out too. I also want to provide a list of small businesses that are located in my town of Sahuarita.

Color scheme

I have decided for my color scheme to follow a desert theme to match the enviroment of my town. I want to have a tan background. I want to use (#fee9b8) as my background tan. I feel that its not too bright but its also not to dark either and would help give a pop of color to my background.

I then want to use this reddish brown color (#e04b2d) as the color for bars and tabs on my page. I plan to have white letters in the brown tabs to help with readability.

For my footer bar I think I want to use a green that would represent the cacti that thrive in my town. The green would be (#2E6E41FF). It's a dark green but it has a bit of life in it which represents the cacti well.
